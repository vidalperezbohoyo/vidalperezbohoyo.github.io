Building your own tool for G-Arm

G-Arm is thought to be an arm to which you can attach different tools. Little by little more are being created. You can also make your own in a very simple way.

First of all, you need to know the attachement dimensions

The end-effect of the arm, has a custom shape to attach any tool. This must be the same for all the tools that you want to connect. The first part of the design is adaptor design.
